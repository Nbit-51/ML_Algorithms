02 · Platform

What ChargeScape AI Does

Six core intelligence modules built for infrastructure planning at national scale.

🏜️

EV Charging Desert Detection

Identifies underserved zones by cross-referencing vehicle registration density, charging station placement, and road corridor data across 847 monitored zones.

📈

Demand Forecasting

Projects EV charging demand 24 months ahead using time-series ML models trained on registration growth, fuel costs, and government adoption incentives.

🧠

Infrastructure Planning Intelligence

Generates ranked placement recommendations with grid capacity constraints, land availability scoring, and ROI timelines for each proposed charger location.

🗺️

Heatmap Visualization

Interactive, sub-district level heatmaps overlaying EV density, charging coverage, and desert severity indices — filterable by state, highway corridor, or risk tier.

⚡

Charger Reliability Insights

Tracks uptime rates, peak utilization windows, and failure patterns across existing stations — feeding reliability scores back into desert severity models.

💰

ROI-Based Expansion Suggestions

Computes payback periods and utilization projections for proposed stations, enabling CPOs and government bodies to prioritize the highest-impact investments.
